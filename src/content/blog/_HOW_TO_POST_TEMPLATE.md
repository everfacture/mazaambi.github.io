## How to Publish a Post Directly from GitHub

1. **Create a new file** in the `src/content/blog/` folder.
2. Name it `your-post-title.md` (no spaces, use dashes).
3. **Copy the Frontmatter** (the block of text between the `---` lines at the top of this file) and paste it into your new file.
4. Update the `title`, `pubDatetime`, and `description`.
5. Remove the `draft: true` line (or set it to `false`).
6. **Write your content** down here.
7. Click the green **Commit changes** button.

The site will automatically rebuild and be live in 60 seconds.

*(Note: AstroPaper automatically calculates and displays the reading time based on your word count. You do not need to add it manually).*
