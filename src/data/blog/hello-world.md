## What This Is

A stream of consciousness. Thoughts captured throughout the day, filtered through questions, published when they deserve it.

## Topics

- Language learning (Indonesian, English)
- UFC fight analysis
- Manchester United suffering
- Business ideas (BGN, commodities)
- Travel and flight hacks
- Surabaya food
- Life lessons

## The Stream

Most thoughts won't become blog posts. They'll live in a daily stream - voice notes, half-formed ideas, things that might become something. The blog is what survives that process.

## How It Works

1. **Throughout the day** - Voice messages, notes, observations
2. **End of day** - Questions to jog memory (3-20 depending on activity)
3. **Raw answers** - My words, not synthesized
4. **Publish or skip** - My choice
5. **Format and deploy** - Technical stuff handled automatically
