Hi, I'm **Mazaambi**.

This is a stream of consciousness on language learning, UFC, Manchester United, business ideas, travel, food, and life lessons from Surabaya, Indonesia.

## What This Is

A digital garden of thoughts. Some posts are polished essays. Others are raw streams captured at the end of a busy day. All are mine.

## Topics

- **Language Learning** - Indonesian, English, and the spaces between
- **UFC** - Fight analysis, predictions, the spectacle
- **Manchester United** - The eternal hope, the constant disappointment
- **Business Ideas** - BGN schemes, commodities, opportunities spotted
- **Travel & Flights** - Trip plans, hacks, adventures
- **Food** - Surabaya eats, recipes, cravings
- **Life & Lessons** - Personal reflections, troubles, growth

## The Stream

Not everything becomes a blog post. Some thoughts stay in the stream - quick notes, half-formed ideas, things that might become something later. The blog captures what survives that process.
